Nanoblog

26 June 2022

This night, i developed a Twitter alternative for personal websites called Nanoblog!

Nanoblog can be used the same way as Zonelets: writing a post in HTML, and the best thing about it, is that it's 100% only pure HTML and CSS!

Note that Nanoblog can be modified in any style you want! You can use the old good Windows 98 style, or you can add a YouTube player, you can do anything you want! Just don't overuse JS!
