4 July 2022 (OMG HAPPY INDEPENDENCE DAY USA!1!!1!1!!!11)

Hi, this will be another long blog post regrading to this site's future

But why?

After reading the OpenBooks Manifesto, I understood that personal web is NOT nostalgia, and the point of the personal web is not to bring back Web 1.0, it's about building a better internet without being owned by companies who puts stuff related to Crypto, NFT and Web3 that nobody even cares now, man, i wish if the entire planet, and not just web, wasn't owned by companies, and there will be more independent organizations.

Cool, but explain what will change

Ok...

  • I will make the site more modern, but without JS
  • A plaintext version (for plaintext browsers as well as the legacy ones)
  • I won't bring my nanoblog back because it'll be impossible for me to recover that small amount of posts (i just wanted to change the damn codebase and i accidentally deleted it, what an idiot), but you can still make your own one!
  • A link directory
  • Probably a beginners guide on joining the personal web
  • Shrines with true Geocities style (ft. legacy html)
  • A manifesto
  • Going outside for good (just kidding i always go outside)
  • If I finally learn stuff about drawing, i'll probably make art of my fursona and put them on an art showcase
  • More blog posts
